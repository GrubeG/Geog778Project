Wisconsin Voting Districts

Every 10 years, as a result of the U.S. Census, Wisconsin has to complete the redistricting process. When ballots are created for election, the races are determined by different districts.

To get started, click on the specific district information that you want to see. For local districts, search first for the county or municipality to zoom directly to that location. To view district, click on the eye icon. For those districts that are grayed out, zoom to appropriate level to be able to see them.
